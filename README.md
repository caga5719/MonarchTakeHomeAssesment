# Project/Document Title: Monarch Invoice Accounting Platform

## 1. Introduction
- **Overview:** This is an Invoice Accounting Platform with a main focus to correctly classifiy and store general ledger items while offering a user friendly visualization dashboard. 
- **Objectives:** Extract necessary accounting information (specifically general ledger items) from pdf invoices and correctly assign them against their general ledger catagories while allowing users to interact with the accounting data from different viewpoints. 
- **Scope:** To extract accounting data from Amazon Business Invoices PDFs with a focus on classifying general ledger information and tying it to necessary purchase orders, properties, and purchasers. This platform will simplify the accounting data entry process for management teams but will not interact with the Yardi Property Management to upload accounting data.
